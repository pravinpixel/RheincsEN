STRATEGIC SOURCING SIMPLIFIED

MeRLIN is our Strategic Sourcing Solution that offers a seamlessly integrated sourcing process automation with supplier relationship management and planning functions augmented by advanced analytics functions, hosted either on premise or on cloud, delivered through Web and Mobile platform.

What is MeRLIN?

MeRLIN is modular, flexible, configurable, and easy to deploy. It is the go-to solution for strategic needs of enterprise sourcing organizations.

Supplier Relationship

Maintain Supplier base throughout the life cycle from on boarding new Suppliers through the configurable approval process, scaling up/down their scope of supply, rating and maintaining their performance and removal from supply chain.

eProcurement

Float online global RFQ’s for parts across multiple commodities to chosen global suppliers, receive online responses, compare responses and make awards.

Send new RFQ’s for non-design price changes and engineering price changes and receive comparative responses.

Analytics

Empower sourcing stakeholders by making the complete sourcing data including Budget, Actuals, Forecast, Awards, Variances, BoM evaluation available at your fingertips.

Summarize, slice and dice data through self-service data explorer, create custom reports and perform what-if analytics.

Master Data Handling

Create a single source of truth by collating disparate data spread across global locations, take advantage of global competitiveness, mitigate risks and run global sourcing programs.

Why MeRLIN?

Who will benefit from MeRLIN

Disparate Sourcing

Discrete Manufacturing Enterprises that have large number of global suppliers supplying to multiple manufacturing locations and involving global sourcing with multi-currency programs will reap significant benefits.

Large Buy vs Build

Enterprises whose bottom line will have significant impact based on savings from sourcing functions will be able to leverage MeRLIN’s powerful analytics to drive cost savings.

Quick TAT for customer RFI

OEM’s that frequently need to respond to customer RFI’s with competitive pricing and other terms can use MeRLIN’s online quote eSourcing function to receive reliable Supplier quotes before responding to customer RFI’s.

Data driven Sourcing

Data driven sourcing behaviour coupled with democratized data access will empower sourcing leaders to be agile in decision making while remaining fully compliant with prevailing regulations.

What are the tangible benefits?

Faster Time to Respond

A fully online eSourcing module speeds up the receipt of Supplier quotes and in turn shortens the time required to respond to customer RFI’s. With the same available bandwidth, the sourcing function can improve the top-line by participating in more customer programs.

Improved Cost Savings

MeRLIN’s powerful Spend Analytics visually represents spend across categories and suppliers at the lowest possible granularity. Buyers can leverage the global visibility of part prices for better negotiation and broader sourcing options.

Risk Mitigation

Availability of online data on Supplier commodity contribution, ratings etc. mitigates the risk of dependency on a few concentrated supplier base.

Focused Supplier Relationship

With the continuous availability of Supplier data, executives can now focus on strategic suppliers to improve market competitiveness. Powerful Spend Analytics provides improved visibility on actual savings provided by Suppliers.
